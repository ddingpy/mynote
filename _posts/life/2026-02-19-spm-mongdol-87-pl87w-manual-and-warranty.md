## SPM MONGDOL 87 (PL87W) Manual & Warranty

**Model:** PL87W
**Product Name:** MONGDOL 87
**Type:** Tri-Mode Custom Keyboard

---

## 1. Function Buttons & Component Layout (Page 2)

### Top Controls

* **Type-C USB Port**
* **Connection Mode Switch**

  * OFF (Wired)
  * Bluetooth
  * 2.4G Wireless

### OS Mode Switch (Mac / Win)

Move the OS switch to change the operating system mode.

* **Windows Mode**

  * Indicator LED blinks **white** twice
* **macOS Mode**

  * Indicator LED blinks **yellow** twice

### Key Behavior Differences

* **Win Key**

  * Works as **Option** key in macOS mode
  * Disabled when Windows key lock is enabled

* **Alt Key**

  * Works as **Command** key in macOS mode

| -------------- | ----------------------------- | --------------------------------------- |

## 5. Charging Warning (Page 6)

⚠ **Charging Precautions**

* Do **not** use a phone charger or charging dock.
* Always charge by connecting the included cable directly to a computer USB port.

## 6. Warranty & Service Policy (Page 7)

### 1) Warranty Period

* SPM provides **1 year warranty** for customers in Korea.
* For other regions, warranty responsibility lies with the local distributor.

### 2) Warranty Exclusions

Warranty becomes void if damage occurs due to:

* Accidents
* Unauthorized modification
* Misuse

### 3) Post-Warranty Service

* Even after warranty expires, SPM will continue offering paid repair support.
* If service is unavailable, SPM will provide guidance for self-repair.
* Responsibility for self-repair lies entirely with the customer.

### 4) Proof of Purchase Required

To begin warranty service, customers must provide valid proof of purchase such as:

* Receipt
* Invoice
