Most people fail at learning not because of low motivation, but because the plan is too heavy.

## A Simple Weekly System

- 3 sessions per week
- 45 minutes per session
- 1 concrete deliverable each week

Examples of weekly deliverables:

- One short blog post
- One code snippet with explanation
- One architecture diagram

## 45-Minute Session Format

- 10 min: review notes from previous session
- 25 min: deep focus on a single topic
- 10 min: summarize in your own words

## Why This Works

- Small scope reduces burnout.
- Repetition improves retention.
- Public artifacts (posts/snippets) create accountability.

## Avoid This Trap

Do not keep collecting tutorials without output. Learning without shipping quickly becomes passive entertainment.
